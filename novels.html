Title

The Dark Woods Demigod

Book Summary

Alora has spent her life in the Dark Woods -- a place where no one goes -- listening to the trees. Most literally listening to them, seeing as she can hear and speak with them. That is until two strangers arrive in the nearby town of Litwafs. Life altering events follow suit and now her world is turned upside down. Can she trust these strangers, will she ever live a calm life again, will she even survive this fight?

Title

The Tyrant King's Three - Secret Weapons

Book Summary

The tyrant king has always ruled the lands with cruelty since he took over. Especially when he takes innocent people from their homes and forces them to compete in his gladiatorial battles for his entertainment. Mara, Aramis, and Drystan have been selected for the king's battles, and now must choose to make allies or enemies out of each other and survive the arena. But this new life they have to adapt reveals many hidden truths and old secrets.

Title

Shattered Fates

Book Summary

The storytellers -- Myth, Legend, Fable, and Lore -- are seers and mystics that have provided council to the kings and queens of Kilroc for hundreds of years. Rune -- the storyteller known as Lore -- is starting to have dreams that she is beginning to think are visions. Gwenore is a princess making her fist political visit for the alliance between her kingdom of Balthru and the kingdom of Kilroc. Despite the rules set upon them the two girls become close and with the help of their friends they begin to see the secrets that plague the lands of Kilroc.
