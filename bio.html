Pennsylvanian resident Caroline Hartman is an author and poet. Her main genre is fantasy, of which she has many works in different stages of progress. She also has a collection of poems and short stories. She enjoys Dungeons and Dragons, reading as much fiction as possible, and watching movies and tv. Influences that can at times be seen in her writing.
